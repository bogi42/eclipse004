# Afterword

Thank you for your interest in my post apocalyptic world! I do hope you enjoyed your time
with Alaric and the gang. 

For me, this was a hard book to write. I almost shied away from the death of Lyra---but
this is a harsh and brutal world, and Lyra’s death has always been a part of Alaric’s
character arc. Don't hate me too much for it, please!

I initially planned five books, but right now, we might look at six books: I'm not sure
I can cram the ending I want to have all into one book. I might have to split the plot
into two more books to give the story its ending.

And that *will* be the ending. I'm not looking into writing this series forever, I'd much
rather start something new after the Dawn of the Eclipse is finished. I'm not a big fan of
series that just go on and on and can never find an ending.

My English readers had to actually wait a long time for this book: After writing book 3,
I started translating the first three books into German. And it turns out: I like the flow
of my writing in German much better. So, this time around, I actually wrote the German
book first, *then* translated it into English (instead of the other way around).

Sorry to keep you waiting, but I had to do this to truly follow my vision for the story.
I wasn't aware of this when I started writing the series, but it turns out that my
plotting is tighter when I do it in German. Who would have thought? There might even be
a German word for this phenomena, or I could invent one.

Maybe Muttersprachenbevorzugungssyndrom? Yeah, that sounds about right.

Anyway, as always, I would be overjoyed if you leave a rating, maybe even a review, on
Amazon. It's the best way for an Indie author like myself to gather more readers. Thank
you!
