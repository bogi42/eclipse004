# Persons
## About the Author

Stefan Bogdanski likes to throw his readers into the dark and harsh reality of a grim
system apocalypse, where survival is a brutal fight and the border between human and
monster becomes hard to see. Every level up eradicates it a bit more.

He knows a bit about actual role-playing mechanics, having been part of the German
Pen'n'Paper RPG system called LodlanD; it even was a post-apocalyptic system, playing
under water on a frozen Earth. He's been a player and dungeon master for over 30 years at
this point, too. All of that experience drives his character-based story telling.

If you're not afraid of the dark side of your level ups (it *is* an apocalypse, after
all), then you've come to the right place. Decisions mean something here, and not every
decision is clearly wrong or right.

*Join the adventure and experience the meaning of levelling up in a world gone crazy!*
