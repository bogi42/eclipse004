# Frontrunner

It doesn't take me long to find the next breach in the city walls. It's as I've feared:
One more grenadier with one of those strange energy weapons has torn another hole into the
wall. Lucky for me---and everyone behind me---, those weapons seem to be rare. My knowledge
about adversarial tech is limited, if I'm being honest, but it seems like those kind of
weapons can't be mass produced.

Or maybe not simply anyone can handle them, and it takes a special boy to actually use
them.

So far, not a single soul has noticed me, running atop the wall. I feel conflicted about
it, but I use an Umbral Volley to quickly kill the guy *and* destroy his equipment at the
same time. I just hope no one has seen me use the spell: I don't want to be taken for an
enemy *again*---I've already had enough of those shenanigans during the early days of the
integration. All that mistrust, and I'm weary and tired of it.

At least, back then, my shadow powers were somewhat of a mystery to the people. Something
they didn't recognize. Fat chance of that happening now---the attackers have clearly
demonstrated the shadow attribute to the citizens of Hope. Thanks a lot for hat, ass hats!

But the defenders are actually to busy with---well, defending their city, to notice what
I'm doing. A battle is raging this side of the wall. I grip my gun and go to work, careful
not to let any dark powers slip.
